What is CommUnity?

CommUnity is an application customized for specific communities with the goal of strengthening your community in all, and new aspects. We consider ourselves as a lifestyle application that provides people the opportunity to have a more luxurious lifestyle in new & existing ways.

How Does it Work?

In registered CommUnity residential areas, we will issue Non Fungible Tokens to Verified Property Owners (VPO's). Those who have the NFT have access to subscriptions that offer the many benefits fit for that community. VPO's will also have access to free benefits without any subscriptions.

Our Goals

  • Redefine what Community means and how we interact with each other.
  • Provide people with a better community experience individually and collectively.
  • Create experiences and development tethered to specific communities
  • Educate users as we move into the world of Web 3.0
  • Allow all CommUnity members to have a say in further CommUnity development.
